# EstateFlow Tenant Application Features

This document outlines the features available to a tenant user within the EstateFlow real estate management application.

## 1. Dashboard
The central hub for a quick overview of your business operations.
- **Financial Snapshots**: View key financial metrics including Total Revenue, Total Inflow (cash received), Total Outflow (cash paid), and Net Cash Flow.
- **Profitability Metrics**: Track Gross Profit (Revenue - Project Expenses) and Actual Profit (Revenue - All Expenses).
- **Project Overview**: Select a project from a dropdown to see a detailed summary of its financials, including revenue, expenses, cash flow, and profit/loss.
- **Customer Overview**: Select a customer to view their purchase history, total sale value, amount paid, and outstanding dues.
- **Notice Board**: View important announcements and notices specific to your tenant account.

## 2. Project Management
Comprehensive tools to manage your real estate projects from start to finish.
- **Create & Edit Projects**: Add new projects with details such as name, location, target sell value, status (Upcoming, Ongoing, Completed), and expected handover date.
- **Manage Flats**: For each project, you can add, edit, and remove individual flats, specifying their name/number and size in square feet.
- **View Project Details**: A dedicated page for each project shows a detailed financial breakdown and a list of all flats with their sale status (Available or Sold).

## 3. Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
A centralized database for all your clients.
- **Add & Manage Customers**: Create and maintain profiles for each customer, including their name, phone number, address, and National ID (NID).
- **View Customer Details**: Each customer has a detail page showing their purchase history, payment progress for each property, and a complete log of all payments made.

## 4. Sales Management
Streamline the process of selling properties.
- **Record Flat Sales**: Create detailed sale records for each flat, linking a project, a flat, and a customer.
- **Detailed Financials**: For each sale, you can record the total amount, price per square foot, parking price, utility costs, booking money, and monthly installment details.
- **Additional Costs**: Add any number of extra costs for modifications or other charges to a sale record.
- **Document Linking**: Attach a link to the official deed document for easy access.

## 5. Payment & Receipt Management
Track all incoming funds from customers.
- **Record Payments**: Log every payment received from a customer for a specific sale. Details include amount, date, payment type (Booking Money, Installment), and payment method (Cash, Cheque, Bank Transfer).
- **Generate Receipts**: Automatically generate and print professional money receipts for any payment transaction. The receipt includes your organization's logo and details.
- **Payment History**: A comprehensive log of all payments is available on the main payments page and on each customer's detail page.

## 6. Expense & Outflow Management
Keep a close eye on all your business expenditures.
- **Track Project Expenses**: Record expenses tied to specific projects, including the vendor, category, amount, quantity, and date.
- **Manage Expense Payments (Pay Bill)**: Log payments made to vendors for specific bills. The system tracks the status of each expense (Unpaid, Partially Paid, Paid) and the remaining due amount.
- **Expense Categories**: Create and manage your own categories for expenses (e.g., Raw Materials, Labor, Marketing).

## 7. Operating Cost Management
Manage general business costs that aren't tied to a specific project.
- **Record Operating Costs**: Log expenses like office rent, salaries, and utilities.
- **Cost Categories**: Create and manage categories for your operating costs to better organize your finances.

## 8. Vendor Management
Maintain a directory of all your suppliers and vendors.
- **Vendor Profiles**: Create and manage profiles for each vendor, including contact person, enterprise name, and phone number.
- **View Vendor Details**: Each vendor has a detail page that summarizes their total billed amount, total paid, and total due, along with a history of all associated expenses and payments.

## 9. Organization Profile
Configure your company's branding and information.
- **Company Details**: Set your company name, website, phone, email, and address. This information is automatically used on documents like payment receipts.
- **Logo Upload**: Add your company's logo URL to be displayed on receipts.

## 10. Security & Access Control
- **Tenant Isolation**: Your data is completely separate from other tenants. You can only access the information associated with your tenant ID.
- **User Accounts**: The system administrator can create multiple user accounts for your organization, each with their own login credentials.
