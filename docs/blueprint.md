# **App Name**: EstateFlow

## Core Features:

- Admin Tenant Management: Admin interface at domain/gopon to add, manage, and disable tenant accounts.
- Tenant Authentication: Authentication flow requiring tenant ID before login at the primary domain.
- Project Management: Add, view, edit, and delete real estate projects with details like name, location, target sell, flats, dates, status, and expected handover date.
- Customer Management: Add, view, edit, and delete customer information including name, phone number, address.
- Flat Sale Management: Link projects, flats, and customers to record sale details: amount, price per sft, parking price, utility cost, notes, deed link, booking money, and monthly installment.
- Payment Tracking: Record payments, link to project, flat, and customer, and log payment details (amount, type, reference, date).
- Receipt Generation: Generate printable A4 receipts with organization logo, company name, website, address, phone, and email.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#293B5F) to convey professionalism and reliability, fitting for real estate management.
- Background color: Light gray (#F2F4F7), a very low saturation of the primary color, for a clean, modern look.
- Accent color: Soft teal (#3C6E71), analogous to blue, to highlight key actions and information.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif, to bring a precise, contemporary feel to headings. Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, combining a modern look and readability for on-screen body text.
- Use clean, professional icons to represent project statuses, customer actions, and payment types.
- Maintain a clear, structured layout with consistent spacing and alignment to facilitate easy navigation and data comprehension.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance user experience without distracting from core functionalities.
